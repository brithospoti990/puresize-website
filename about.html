About PureSize.

We are not a retail chain. We are engineers and specialists who grew frustrated watching homeowners waste thousands of dollars on wrong-sized equipment.

Founder & Lead Engineer

Rex Taylor

Rex began his career installing water treatment systems across the American Southwest in the early 2000s — a region infamous for some of the hardest water in the country. Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa: he has crawled under more homes and installed more systems than most people have seen in a lifetime.

After years of watching homeowners get oversold by big-box stores and locked into overpriced dealer contracts, Rex built PureSize with a single mission: give people the exact same sizing data that professionals use, for free.

Today, Rex and his review board — a team of licensed Water Quality Association (WQA) specialists — maintain PureSize's algorithms, test equipment in our Phoenix lab, and ensure every recommendation is backed by real engineering, not sales incentives.
